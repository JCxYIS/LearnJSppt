Why Javascript?

  • Easy to Learn

    Very similar to C language (Object-Oriented), and it's really "Coder-Friendly". You don't even have to type ; at every ends of lines!

  • Easy to Use

    No need to compile, just write it inside a HTML file! Also, hit F12 to debug!

    There are tons of extensions for JS (e.g. JQuery, Phaser), letting JS be able to do almost anything!
